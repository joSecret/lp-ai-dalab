Education technology has the power to transform how people learn. We build platforms that make education more accessible, engaging, and effective.

## Accessibility & Scale

We build to WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards and have experience scaling EdTech platforms to serve millions of concurrent learners across multiple time zones.
